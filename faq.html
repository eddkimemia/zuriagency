Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZuriAgency. Can't find an answer? Contact our 24/7 support.

01 General Questions

Is ZuriAgency a pyramid scheme?
No. ZuriAgency is a digital community platform that rewards members for referrals and team building. Unlike pyramid schemes, we provide a transparent dashboard, real-time tracking, and a reserve fund to ensure all payouts are fulfilled within 24 hours.
Is it legal in Kenya?
Yes. ZuriAgency operates as a digital marketing and community referral platform. We comply with Kenyan digital commerce standards and use secure M-Pesa integration for all transactions.

02 Earnings & Payments

How do I get paid?
All earnings are paid directly to your registered M-Pesa number. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours of request, though most members receive their funds much faster.
What if I don't refer anyone?
While referring others is the primary way to earn high commissions, you still benefit from being part of the community. However, to maximize your KES 1,000 investment, we recommend active participation and team building.

03 Getting Started

How do I join?
Click "Get Started", register your details, and make a one-time activation payment of KES 1,000. You'll receive your unique referral link immediately.
