01 General Questions

Is ZuriAgency a pyramid scheme? ↓
No. ZuriAgency is a legitimate digital community that rewards members for referring others and building networks. Unlike pyramid schemes, our system is transparent, everyone has equal opportunity to earn, and we maintain a reserve fund to ensure all members are paid.
Is ZuriAgency legal in Kenya? ↓
Yes. ZuriAgency operates as a digital referral and community platform. We comply with all relevant Kenyan laws and encourage responsible participation.
Do I need any special skills to join? ↓
No special skills are required. The platform is designed to be simple and user-friendly. If you can use WhatsApp or M-Pesa, you can use ZuriAgency.

02 Earnings & Referrals

How much can I really earn? ↓
Your earnings depend on your effort. Many members earn between KES 10,000 – 50,000+ per month by actively growing their network. Some top performers earn significantly more through multiple levels of referrals.
What if I don’t refer anyone? ↓
You can still join and benefit from the platform. However, members who actively share their referral link and build a team see the best and fastest results.
How does the referral bonus work? ↓
You earn KES 350 instantly for every person you directly refer. You also earn KES 150 from every referral made by people in your network (upline income). There is no limit to how much you can earn.

03 Payments & Security

How do I get paid? ↓
All earnings are paid directly to your M-Pesa number within 24 hours of confirmation. There are no delays or complicated withdrawal processes.
Is it safe to invest my money? ↓
Yes. We maintain a reserve fund to ensure all members are paid on time. Your data and transactions are protected with enterprise-grade security.
What is the minimum payout amount? ↓
There is no minimum withdrawal amount. You can request to withdraw your earnings as soon as they are available in your account.

04 Getting Started

How do I get started? ↓
Simply click “Get Started”, fill in your details, and activate your account with a one-time payment of KES 1,000. You’ll receive your referral link immediately after activation.
Is there a free trial or discount? ↓
Yes! New members who join today receive a FREE KES 500 activation credit. This reduces your effective activation cost.
Can I join using any phone? ↓
Yes. ZuriAgency is fully mobile-friendly and works on any smartphone or feature phone. No app download is required.
