FAQ

Still Have Questions?

Is the KES 1,000 a one-time payment?

Yes, absolutely. You pay KES 1,000 only once to activate your account. After that, there are no recurring fees, no monthly subscriptions, no hidden charges. Every shilling you earn goes straight to your M-Pesa.

What happens if I can't find anyone to refer?

Your upline and the community are there to support you. We provide training, marketing materials, and strategies to help you grow. Plus, even one referral earns you KES 350 — that's already a 35% return on your KES 1,000.

How quickly do I receive my money?

Earnings are processed within 24 hours of being confirmed. In most cases, members report receiving their M-Pesa within a few hours. There's no minimum withdrawal threshold — even KES 150 gets paid out.

Can I earn from people I didn't directly refer?

Yes! That's the beauty of the upline bonus. When someone you referred brings in a new member, you automatically receive KES 150. This is passive income — you don't need to do anything extra.

What if the person I refer doesn't activate?

No problem at all. They simply won't be counted as an active referral until they activate. You can follow up with them, or focus on referring others who are ready to take action. Your link never expires.
