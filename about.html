Our Journey

The ZuriAgency Story

From a WhatsApp group to a platform serving thousands. Here's how it happened.

January 2023

It Started With a WhatsApp Group

Our founder, frustrated with stagnant bank interest rates, created a small WhatsApp group with 15 friends. The idea was simple: pool resources, share opportunities, and earn together. Within 2 weeks, everyone had made a profit.

March 2023

The First 100 Members

Word spread fast. Friends told friends. The group hit 100 members, and the manual tracking became impossible. That's when we knew we needed a proper platform — not just a group chat.

June 2023

Platform Launch

After 3 months of development, ZuriAgency.com went live. Automated tracking, instant M-Pesa payouts, referral links — everything was now seamless. Membership doubled in the first month.

November 2023

KES 1 Million Milestone

We celebrated our first KES 1 million in total payouts. 500+ active members. Stories pouring in of school fees paid, businesses started, and lives changed. This was our proof of concept — and our motivation to go bigger.

March 2024

All 47 Counties

ZuriAgency members now span every county in Kenya. From Mombasa to Lodwar, from Kisumu to Garissa. We built a truly national community — powered entirely by word of mouth.

2025 — Now

2,500+ Members. KES 4.8M+ Paid. Growing Fast.

We're just getting started. New features, tier upgrades, East African expansion, and a mobile app are all in the pipeline. The best time to join was yesterday. The second best time is now.
